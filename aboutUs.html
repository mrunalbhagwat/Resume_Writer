About Us

Welcome to A & H ALLIANCE SDN. BHD., where innovation meets excellence. Founded on March 11, 2014, and incorporated under the Companies Act 1965, we are a Private Limited Company based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Our journey began with a mission to bridge the gap between talent and opportunity by offering comprehensive manpower recruitment, placement, and pre-departure training services. Over the years, we have proudly served multiple industries across South East Asia and Middle East.

Today, our expertise extends far beyond recruitment. Through our subsidiaries and partnerships, we have diversified into cutting-edge technology solutions, workforce empowerment, and strategic consulting, making us a trusted name across multiple industries.
